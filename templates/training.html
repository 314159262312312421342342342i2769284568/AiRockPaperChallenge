Instructions

You need to collect at least 30 images of each gesture (rock, paper, scissors) to train the AI effectively.

  1. Select a gesture type from the buttons below
  2. Position your hand in front of the camera
  3. Click "Capture Image" to capture a sample of your gesture
  4. Repeat for different hand positions and angles
  5. After collecting enough samples, click "Train Model"
Make sure to vary your hand position, angle, and distance from the camera for better training results.
